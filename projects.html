Tweet Sort

www.tweetsort.io
GitHub link

JavaScript, Node.js, Express, Twitter API, React

Fetches a twitter user by username and returns a list of the last ~3200 tweets in the user-specified, sorted order. I love lurking on the top of all time posts on subreddits, especially when I'm trying to learn about something new. On Twitter, you can follow such intelligent and creative people. I wanted to see what their most well received tweets were.
